What is Anxiety?


A feeling of worry or unease, typically about an imminent event or something with an uncertain outcome. You might feel anxious when faced with a problem at work, before taking a test, or making an important decision!

How Do We Combat Anxiety?


The Five Steps of AWARE!
Acknowledge and Accept
Wait and Watch
Actions
Repeat
End
Let's look at what each step entails!

Acknowledge and Accept


Acknowledge: You acknowledge the present reality. You will not ignore it, or pretend it is not there.
Accept: You accept the fact I am afraid at this moment. You do not fight the feeling!

Wait and Watch

Wait: You don't need to run away to get relief. Let relief come to you.
Watch: Observe your anxiety and get a little distance from your emotions.

Actions


Actions (to make yourself more comfortable):
Breathe! Talk to Yourself! Get Involved in the Present!

Repeat


Repeat: Remind yourself that is OK if it happens! You are going to be fine!

End


End: Your only job is to make yourself as comfortable as possible!

What is the purpose of Shortify Your Stress?


Shortify Your Stress will simplify your life! You feel anxious about a job interview or an upcoming test...you want to calm down. Some of the features include: small chatrooms, relaxing music, and fun DIY (do it yourself) activities. The application is not the only form of treatment you need, but it can help reduce anxiety and restore serenity to your life.
